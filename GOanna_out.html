AgBase GOanna
GOanna allows users to quickly add more GO annotations to their data by transferring GO annotations based on sequence homology. The input file is a text file containing either (1) protein accessions separated by a return/line break; or (2) a fasta file of either nucleotide or protein sequences. You will be notified by email when your GOanna results are ready. Please use the navigation provided by the webpage and not the browser back button.

Retrieve GOanna job status information by clicking here.

Because searching the databases is time consuming, we limit GOanna to 5,000 inputs (accessions or sequences) or an overall file size of 6Mb. For very large jobs, please contact AgBase directly to assist you with your analysis
